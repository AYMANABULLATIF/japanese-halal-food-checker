How to Use the Halal Ingredient Checker

1. Click on "Take a Picture" to capture an image of the product's ingredients.

2. Alternatively, you can upload an image directly from your device.

3. Click "Check Halal Status" to analyze the image for haram or questionable ingredients.

4. The results will be displayed with color-coded highlights indicating the status of each ingredient.
